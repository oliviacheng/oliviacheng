Personal Statement

I want to bridge the gap between technology and people. Most tech being put out these days are only utilized by a portion of the population; there are many communities that technology is advancing beyond and leaving behind. As a designer, my job is to ensure that further development of technology means increased usability and accessibility in all aspects (physically,  financially, mentally, ect). So let’s talk and share ideas that will make people of all backgrounds feel safe and connected.
