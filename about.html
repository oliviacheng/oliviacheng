Who am I? I'm an aspiring designer graduating from UC Berkeley this December!

Why am I here? I'm in awe of how far technology has come and the potential it carries. Design has the power to tip the scales of this potential to be more inclusive and accessible. Designers throw around the word 'empathy' when making a product, but it's near impossible to completely understand an experience until you have experienced it yourself. (And even then it's different). This is a challenge I think everyone should be tackling.

I have strong values. Honesty, integrity, grit, kindness, and teamwork. I don't like to assume or judge.

What about free time? You can find me at the gym hooping with friends, watching NBA, cooking and eating, or kicking back with The Office for the millionth time.

Visions? In the future, I hope to be someone who is capable of helping others whenever and whereever I want. I want to travel the world and understand how different people struggle. I want my work to reflect postitive change in this world.
